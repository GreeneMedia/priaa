FAQs

Listed here are the most commonly asked questions about who we are and how we operate. If you have a question that is not found here, please do not hesitate to contact us and we will be happy to address it.

Is PRIAA CAPITAL FINANCE a lender or a broker?

PRIAA CAPITAL FINANCE is a licensed lender and broker under California Finance Lenders License # 603 L334. PRIAA CAPITAL FINANCE funds non-conforming commercial, residential investment and business loans in markets nationwide. PRIAA CAPITAL FINANCE also functions as a correspondent and preferred origination point for a small group of banks, institutional lenders, investment funds and private finance companies. In states where PRIAA CAPITAL FINANCE is not licensed as a mortgage lender or mortgage broker (and licensing is required), PRIAA CAPITAL FINANCE acts strictly as a loan packager for, or a referral source to, those entities licensed to lend.

Why should I work with PRIAA CAPITAL FINANCE over other lenders?

If you are a broker or borrower looking for financing in this market, you know the process can be difficult and painstaking. The loan programs SECC offers are genuine, competitive (or more than competitive), and they perform. We handle the heavy lifting so that you can focus your efforts on what’s most important to you. We also offer approved partners fee incentives and provide a variety of marketing tools and support.

Does PRIAA CAPITAL FINANCE charge upfront fees?

Once PRIAA CAPITAL FINANCE has analyzed a loan submission and determined its eligibility for financing, a Letter of Interest (LOI) is issued. Upon acceptance and signing of the LOI, the borrower will be required to pay a non-refundable underwriting and/or appraisal fee ranging from $0.00 to $1,995.00 depending on the loan size and complexity of the transaction. Refunds will not be issued on transactions that do not close because of borrower/broker misrepresentation, cancellation, delays, inability to meet documentation or information requests, omission or fraud, or due to adverse results of third-party reports. A previously submitted underwriting or appraisal fee will be returned only in the event that a loan does not close due to PRIAA CAPITAL FINANCE error or failure to perform as outlined in the LOI.

Does PRIAA CAPITAL FINANCE charge loan points and/or fees at closing?

In most situations, PRIAA CAPITAL FINANCE is compensated directly by our investors or from the sale of our loans, thus we do not ordinarily charge the borrower any excessive points or fees. We will always provide a fee quote upfront upon request so there are no surprises for the broker or borrower.

How long does it take to close a loan with PRIAA CAPITAL FINANCE?

Our private money business loans close in as fast as 1-5 days. It takes approximately 2-3 weeks to close a non-conforming commercial or non-owner occupied residential loan transaction. SBA transactions can take anywhere from 60-120 days.

Does PRIAA CAPITAL FINANCE issue forward commitments?

No. A loan commitment will never be issued for your transaction unless it is presently ready for closing and funding.

Does PRIAA CAPITAL FINANCE offer loan programs outside of the United States?

We offer loan programs in all 50 states, and most domestic primary, secondary and tertiary U.S. markets. We do not offer any loan programs in any other countries, other than Canada via our private business financing program.

What collateral will PRIAA CAPITAL FINANCE lend against?

Only commercial real estate, 1 to 4 unit non-owner occupied residential properties held (or to be acquired) for investment or business purposes, businesses and business assets are eligible for financing under our programs. We do not offer financing on owner occupied residential properties, securities or any other types of collateral.

How do I get approved as a partner with PRIAA CAPITAL FINANCE?

Contact us or your PRIAA CAPITAL FINANCE account executive to let us know that you would like to be set up as a partner, and we will forward the broker registration package necessary for your approval. There is no cost to register but you must pass a background check screening.
